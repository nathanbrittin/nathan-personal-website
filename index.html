DNA to RNA Transcript Code

Written in Python.

This script will take a DNA seqeunce (in a text file) of any length and then convert it to its RNA equivalent

>>>fp = input("Enter filepath of .txt file: ")

>>>def transcription(fp):

  with open(fp, 'r') as seq:

    dna = seq.read().upper()

    rna = dna.replace('T', 'U')

    print(rna)

>>>transcription(fp)
